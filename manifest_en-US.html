Role-playing GameRPG Manifest

This is a manifesto for a good game of Role-playing Game. We are discovering good practices for maintaining a good RPG table, based on the experience and history of players. These ideals are unrelated to any system of rules, platform (online or in person), format (with or without masters, live action, table) and other conventions. Through this work, we value:

  1. Fun more than rules
  2. Players more than dungeon masters
  3. People more than characters
  4. Proper improvisation more than negligent
  5. Proper planning more than exaggerated

That is, while there is value in the items on the right, we value the items on the left more.
